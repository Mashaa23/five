Let's talk life shall we...

Life is a quality that distinguishes matter that has biological processes, such as signaling and self-sustaining processes, from matter that does not 1. It is defined descriptively by the capacity for homeostasis, organization, metabolism, growth, adaptation, response to stimuli, and reproduction 1. Life is a process, not a substance, and it is a characteristic of all living things 1. The study of life has been ongoing since ancient times, with theories such as materialism and hylomorphism asserting that living things are composed of four eternal elements and have souls, respectively 1. Life originated at least 3.5 billion years ago, resulting in a universal common ancestor
